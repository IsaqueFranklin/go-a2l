PARI functions necessary:

- strtoi, negi (conversion)
- qfi (generate quadratic form)
- nupow (expoent)
- gmul, ginv (multiplication, inverse)
- sqri, mulii, subii, shifti (arthmetic)
- Fp_inv, mpodd (modular)
- gel, diviiexact (access/divisio)
- randomi (randomization)

All of those are made to operate on quadratic class groups.
